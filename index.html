Services

I support hospitality businesses through three core areas of expertise — Opening, Rebuilding, and Transforming. Each service is grounded in people‑first leadership, operational clarity, and cultural alignment.

Opening

For new venues, experiential concepts, and multi‑site operations preparing to launch.

  • Full pre‑opening planning and leadership onboarding
  • Development of SOPs, compliance, H&S, and financial systems
  • Multi‑week training academies and soft‑launch programmes
  • Coordination with build teams, designers, and contractors
  • Labour modelling, rota design, and workforce planning
  • Guest journey design and service‑standard implementation
Proven impact: Opened F1 Arcade Birmingham with 150+ staff, delivering £7m+ first‑year revenue, 30%+ EBITDA, +75 NPS, and a 4.7 Google rating.

Rebuilding

For businesses facing cultural challenges, operational inconsistency, or declining performance.

  • Cultural diagnostics and leadership coaching
  • Team restructuring and recruitment of key roles
  • Operational audits and system stabilisation
  • Implementation of scheduling, stock, and reporting systems
  • Guest experience recovery and service realignment
  • Hands‑on leadership presence to stabilise operations
Proven impact: Rebuilt culture and leadership at Tattu Birmingham, achieving a 5‑star EHO rating, £5.5m revenue, and 25.9% EBITDA.

Transforming

For businesses seeking long‑term strategic growth and operational evolution.

  • Multi‑year cultural and operational transformation strategies
  • Integration of PMS, EPOS, workforce, and stock systems
  • Financial performance improvement and GP optimisation
  • Leadership development and coaching
  • Estate‑wide service and guest‑journey redesign
  • Support with capital investment and long‑term planning
Proven impact: Delivered £900k YoY revenue growth and £1.2m EBITDA at The Peligoni Club while leading estate‑wide system integration and 10‑year investment planning.
