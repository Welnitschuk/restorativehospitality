About Richard Welnitschuk

Restorative Hospitality is led by Richard Welnitschuk, a hospitality executive with more than fifteen years of experience across luxury hotels, award-winning restaurants, multi-site operations and complex hospitality environments.

Throughout his career Richard has specialised in leading teams through periods of growth, transition and change, combining operational expertise with a people-first leadership philosophy.

His approach is rooted in a simple belief: organisations perform at their best when people feel valued, trusted and connected to a shared purpose.

Capability Statement

Restorative Hospitality provides senior-level operational and cultural leadership for hospitality businesses in transition.

Core capabilities include:

  • Pre-opening strategy and execution
  • Operational stabilisation and recovery
  • Leadership coaching and team development
  • Cultural transformation and engagement design
  • Guest experience architecture and service design
  • Interim executive leadership (GM / Ops Director level)

Engagements are tailored, hands-on, and outcome-driven — designed to create measurable improvement in both people experience and commercial performance.
