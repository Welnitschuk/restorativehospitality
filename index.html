Services

Opening

Building the foundations for successful launches.

For new venues, experiential concepts and multi-site operations preparing to launch.

  • Full pre-opening recruitment, leadership and team onboarding
  • Development of SOPs, compliance, H&S, reporting and operating systems
  • Multi-week training academies, media and soft launch programmes
  • Coordination with build teams, designers and contractors
  • Labour modelling, rota design and workforce planning
  • Guest journey design and service standard implementation

Rebuilding

Restoring confidence, culture and performance.

For businesses facing cultural challenges, operational inconsistency or declining performance.

  • Cultural diagnostics and leadership coaching
  • Team restructuring and recruitment of key roles
  • Operational audits and system stabilisation
  • Implementation of operational and reporting systems
  • Guest experience recovery and service realignment
  • Hands-on leadership presence to stabilise operations

Transforming

Creating sustainable long-term growth.

For businesses seeking strategic growth and operational evolution.

  • Multi-year cultural and operational transformation strategies
  • Integration of systems and systemic change
  • Long-term financial performance improvement and GP optimisation
  • Leadership development and coaching
  • Estate-wide service and guest journey redesign
  • Support with capital investment and long-term planning

Interim Leadership

Experienced leadership when it matters most.

  • General Manager cover
  • Operations Director support
  • Regional operational leadership
  • Opening leadership
  • Transformation project leadership
  • Executive mentoring and succession support

Leadership Through Transition

Restorative Hospitality is led by Richard Welnitschuk, a hospitality executive with more than fifteen years of experience across luxury hotels, award-winning restaurants, major openings and complex hospitality operations.

Throughout his career Richard has repeatedly been trusted to lead businesses through periods of growth, change and transformation.

His leadership philosophy is shaped by a simple belief: exceptional hospitality begins with exceptional teams.

By creating environments where people feel valued, trusted and connected to a shared purpose, organisations can achieve lasting cultural and commercial success.
