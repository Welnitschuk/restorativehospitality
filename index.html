Leadership Through Transition

Restorative Hospitality is led by Richard Welnitschuk, a hospitality executive with more than fifteen years of experience leading luxury hotels, award-winning restaurants, major openings and large-scale hospitality operations.

From Gleneagles and Rick Stein to Tattu, F1 Arcade and The Peligoni Club, Richard has consistently helped businesses navigate growth, change and transformation while developing high-performing teams and strong cultures.

His leadership philosophy is rooted in purpose-driven leadership, operational excellence and the belief that exceptional hospitality begins with people.

A Trusted Network of Specialists

Every project is unique.

Alongside core consultancy services, Restorative Hospitality draws upon a trusted network of specialists including executive chefs, bar consultants, revenue experts, learning and development specialists, recruitment partners and guest experience professionals.
