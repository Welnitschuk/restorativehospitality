Services

I support hospitality businesses through three core areas of expertise—Opening, Rebuilding, and Transforming. Each service is designed to strengthen culture, elevate guest experience, and deliver sustainable commercial performance.

Opening

For new hospitality venues, experiential concepts, and multi‑site operations preparing to launch.

  • Full pre‑opening planning including recruitment strategy and leadership onboarding
  • Development of SOPs, financial reporting, compliance, H&S, and stock systems
  • Design and delivery of multi‑week training academies and soft‑launch programmes
  • Coordination with build teams, designers, and contractors during final project stages
  • Labour modelling, rota design, and workforce planning
  • Guest journey design and service‑standard implementation
Proven impact: Successfully opened a 14,000 sq. ft. experiential venue (F1 Arcade) with 150+ staff, achieving £7m+ first‑year revenue, 30%+ EBITDA, +75 NPS, and a 4.7 Google rating.

Rebuilding

For established businesses facing cultural challenges, operational inconsistency, or declining performance.

  • Cultural diagnostics and leadership coaching to rebuild engagement and accountability
  • Team restructuring, recruitment of key roles, and development of progression pathways
  • Operational audits across service, systems, compliance, and financial controls
  • Implementation of scheduling, stock, and reporting systems
  • Guest experience recovery plans and service‑standard realignment
  • Hands‑on leadership presence to stabilise operations
Proven impact: Rebuilt culture and leadership at Tattu Birmingham, improving retention, achieving a 5‑star EHO rating, and delivering £5.5m revenue with 25.9% EBITDA.

Transforming

For businesses seeking long‑term strategic growth, operational evolution, or estate‑wide improvement.

  • Multi‑year operational and cultural transformation strategies
  • Integration of PMS, EPOS, workforce management, and stock systems
  • Financial performance improvement including GP optimisation and labour efficiency
  • Leadership development programmes and coaching
  • Estate‑wide service and guest‑journey redesign
  • Support with capital investment planning and long‑term operational modelling
Proven impact: Delivered £900k YoY revenue growth and £1.2m EBITDA at The Peligoni Club while leading estate‑wide system integration and 10‑year investment planning.
